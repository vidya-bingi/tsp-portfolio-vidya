AimHire

AimHire is a job portal developed by me and my team, designed to connect job seekers with potential employers efficiently. The platform, built using HTML, CSS, React.js, and a MySQL database, offers a seamless user experience for browsing and applying for job opportunities. AimHire aims to simplify the job search process, making it easier for candidates to find their ideal career paths
